All about me.

My name is Ashley and I am from Texas! I am a wife and mother to Michael and Keighton. I have a degree in Design Communications and have always loved design. In College I had to take a Web Developement class and I loved it so much. I never went any further with my developemnt classes after I graduated. I was offered a job at a large school district as their graphic designer, so I did more print than web.

Around 2014 I began to take classes from Treehouse and more recently Skillcrush. I have been brushing up on my skills and learning new things to be able to make a full switch to front-end developement. I look forward to using my design skills and eye for detail to write clean, elegant code.
